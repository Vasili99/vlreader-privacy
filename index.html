Effective date: 2026-01-12 · Support: vlreader.app@gmail.com
Translations (ES/DE) are provided for convenience. In case of discrepancies, the English version prevails.

1) What information we process

Account information

  • Email address
  • User identifier (user ID)
  • Authentication/session data
  • Basic profile information (e.g., subscription plan status)

If you sign in with Google, we may receive basic profile data provided by Google (such as a profile picture/avatar).

Subscriptions & purchases (Google Play)

When you purchase a subscription, we may process:

  • Product identifiers (e.g., pro_monthly, pro_plus_monthly)
  • Purchase token / transaction identifiers required to validate your subscription
  • Subscription status (active/inactive) and the plan assigned to your account (free / pro / pro_plus)

We do not collect or store your payment card details. Payments are processed by Google Play.

Technical information

We may process technical information needed to operate the app securely and reliably (for example, diagnostics when issues occur).

2) How we use your information

  • Create and manage your account
  • Authenticate you and maintain your session
  • Provide app functionality and customer support
  • Enable paid features and verify subscriptions
  • Prevent abuse/fraud and improve security
  • Maintain and improve the app

3) Sharing of information

We share information only as necessary to provide the service:

  • Supabase (authentication and database hosting): stores account and profile data (e.g., email, user ID, subscription plan).
  • Google Play / Google Play Billing: processes payments and provides subscription purchase data required for verification.

We do not sell your personal information.

4) Data retention

We keep your information as long as necessary to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes / enforce agreements. You may request deletion (see Section 6).

5) Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

6) Your rights & requests

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your data. To make a privacy request, contact: vlreader.app@gmail.com

Suggested subject: Privacy Request — VL Reader

7) Children

VL Reader is not intended for children under the minimum age required by applicable law without parental consent. If you believe a child provided personal information, contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

8) International transfers

Our service providers (e.g., Supabase, Google) may process information on servers located outside your country. By using the app, you acknowledge that your information may be processed in other jurisdictions.

9) Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will be available at this URL. If changes are material, we will try to provide appropriate notice.

10) Contact

Support email: vlreader.app@gmail.com
